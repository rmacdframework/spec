# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone interested in the RMACD Framework, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** — Treat all participants with respect and consideration
- **Be constructive** — Provide helpful feedback focused on improving the framework
- **Be collaborative** — Work together to solve problems and share knowledge
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome newcomers and help them participate
- **Be professional** — Maintain a professional tone in all interactions

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting comments, or unconstructive criticism
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project communication channels
- Events or meetups related to the project

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to:

Email: contact@rmacd-framework.org

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is committed to maintaining confidentiality regarding the reporter.
